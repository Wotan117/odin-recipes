Meal Prep Burito

Ingredients

  • 6 Tortillas
  • 900g Ground Beef
  • 250g Onions
  • 300g Canned Tomatoes
  • 120g Cream Cheese
  • 120g Mozarella
  • Salt, Pepper, Oregano, Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Grill beef in pan
  2. Cut onions
  3. Season with salt, pepper and lots of oregano
  4. At medium heat ad pureed canned tomatoes together with 2tes salt and pepper
  5. At cream cheese
  6. Cut and ad mozarella
  7. Roll into tortillas
  8. fry burito in pan

Nutritonal Value

  • 1 Portion
  • Calories = 513kcal
  • Fat = 15g
  • Protein = 46g
