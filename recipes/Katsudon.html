Katsudon

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1/2 Spring Onion
  • 1 Tes Vegetable Broth
  • 1/2 Cup Of Short Grain Rice
  • 1 Tas Sojasauce
  • 100 ml Water
  • 1 Tes Sugar
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 Medium Schnitzel

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Cut onions in slices
  3. cut spring onions
  4. Mix broth with water, sugar and Sojasauce
  5. Heat pan with olive oil and add onions
  6. Remove onions when glassy
  7. Grill schnitzel in pan from bouth sides until gold brown
  8. Take schnityel out of pan and cut in strips and add it back into the pan
  9. add broth mix over the meat
  10. add back onions
  11. add eggs scrambled
  12. turn down heat and let the egss stock a bit before adding everything over the fresh cooked rice
  13. add spring onions on top

Nutritonal Value

  • 1 Portion
  • Calories = 700kcal
  • Fat = 34g
  • Protein = 48g
