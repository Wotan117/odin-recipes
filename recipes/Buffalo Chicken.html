Buffalo Chicken

Ingredients

  • 170g Chicken Breast
  • 100g Red Bell Pepper
  • 50g Onions
  • 30g Cream Cheese
  • 40g Frank Hot Sauce
  • 5g Honey
  • 1g Cornstarch
  • 20g Water
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Put hot sauce into a glass
  2. Add honey
  3. Add water
  4. Add cornstarch
  5. Mix
  6. Sprinkle salt on bouth side of the chicken breast and put it into the fridge for 1h
  7. Cut chicken into cubes
  8. Dice onion and bell pepper
  9. Fry onion and pepper for around 3-5min at medium heat under constant sturing
  10. Push vegetables aside and add chicken
  11. Season with around 2g of garlic powder, 1g of ground cuman and some black pepper
  12. Fry until the chicken is done
  13. Add sauce
  14. Add cream cheese
  15. Mix until creamy

Nutritonal Value

  • 1 Portion
  • Calories = 312kcal
  • Carbs = 17g
  • Fat = 10g
  • Protein = 42g
